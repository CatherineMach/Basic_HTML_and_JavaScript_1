Bumble bees are in massive trouble.

So I hope these facts may bring awareness:)

Bumble bees can detect the quality of pollen in flowers which causes them to be very picky on the flowers they gather nectar from!

During warm summer periods, bumblebees hover at the top of the nest and vibrate their wings in order to circulate the air and maintain a nice cool nest
